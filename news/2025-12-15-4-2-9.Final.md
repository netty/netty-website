We are happy to announce the release of netty 4.2.9.Final. This is a bug-fix release which also fixes a regression introduced by 4.2.8.Final so please consider upgrading as soon as possible.


The most important changes are:

* Fix HTTP startline validation ([#16022](https://github.com/netty/netty/pull/16022))
* Fix MpscIntQueue bug (#16023) ([#16023](https://github.com/netty/netty/pull/16023))


For more details please visit our [bug tracker](https://github.com/netty/netty/issues?q=milestone%3A4.2.9.Final+is%3Aclosed)
